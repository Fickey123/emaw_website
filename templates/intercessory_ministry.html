Intercessory Ministry

Prayer and Fasting

The Intercessory Ministry leads the church in times of dedicated prayer and fasting. We believe in the power of seeking God fervently to break strongholds and align with His will.

Prayer Summits

We host powerful prayer summits that gather believers to intercede for the nation, the church, and individual needs. These summits are filled with prophetic declarations and divine encounters.

Prayer Retreats

Prayer retreats offer a quiet, focused environment where intercessors spend extended time in the presence of God. It’s a time for deep reflection, revelation, and spiritual empowerment.
