Prison Ministry

The Prison ministry is a Christ-centered outreach dedicated to serving the incarcerated population with love, dignity, and compassion.

Grounded in the Word of God and driven by the Great Commission, this ministry recongnizes that every person - regardless of past mistakes - is created in the image of God and deserves hope, forgiveness, and a second chance.
