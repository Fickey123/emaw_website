Introduction

This is a two year part time ministry training program. It is designed to equip the trainees with bibilical mind, theological knowledge, Holistic mind and ministry skills to serve the lord effectively in todays world.

Accreditation

CBC courses are accredited in the united states by our affiliated CRIO Bible College which offers Diploma in Biblical Studies founded by Dr. Ron Hesset.

CRIO Bible College has established training centres in various regions to make the programs accessible to as many students as possible. These centres are staffed with qualified instructors and facilitators committed to your spiritual and academic growth.
