Our Ministry

Dear friend,

I am so greatful to God for all our intercessors, members, friends and partners.

Through your Prayers, Support and Love, we have enjoyed seeing the realization of our mission unfold.

Since the inception of Evangelicam Missions Across the World(EMAW) Ministry on 30/8/2009, the Lord has been gracious in enabling us to achieve our mission through evangelism and discipleship.

Several churches have been established in different counties in kenya.

Through the proclamation of the word of Lord Jesus Christ, we have witnessed worst people in the community being changed to the best, sinners being forgiven and receiving Jesus Christ, families being reconciled, sick being healed, youths being joined in Holy matrimony and other many miracles, signs and wonders.

Its my pleasure to invite you to join us in the ministry of our Lord Jesus Christ, to reach the unreached with the Gospel.

Departments

1. Youth Department
The purpose of this department is to promote fellowship and socialization among the young people in the church. They are under the patron and lead by the chairperson who is appointed by the church pastor.
2. Women Department
It is lead by a chairlady who is appointed by the church pastor. She is responsible to mobilize the ladies to have unity and improve their spiritual standards.
3. Men's Department
It is lead by one of the decons appointed by the pastor who is responsible to unite all men and encourage them to participate in the events of building the entire church.
4. Sunday School Department
It is divided into two; adults and children sunday school. The pastor appoints teachers to take care of the children and make sure they understand the true doctrine of the bible.
5. Missionary & Evangelism Department
The church has mission programs both locally and internationally whereby any mission outside the country works under the executive board committee and missionaries are trained on the skills of the ministry.
6. Social Welfare Department
It caters for the physical needs of the community in the church or around the church. There are elected office bearers for the department and the committee can receive funds from the members.
7. Instruments Department
The pastor appoints the person in charge. Instruments to be hired or borrowed will be authorized by the executive local church committee under pastor's consultation.
8. Music Department
It caters for praise and worship in the church and ensures proper coordination for each group to exercise their talent. It consits of bands, choirs, worship team and junior sunday school worshippers.
9. Ushering Department
It caters for praise and worship in the church and ensures proper coordination for each group to exercise their talent. It consits of bands, choirs, worship team and junior sunday school worshippers.
10. Media Department
It caters for praise and worship in the church and ensures proper coordination for each group to exercise their talent. It consits of bands, choirs, worship team and junior sunday school worshippers.
11. Intercessory Department
It caters for praise and worship in the church and ensures proper coordination for each group to exercise their talent. It consits of bands, choirs, worship team and junior sunday school worshippers.
