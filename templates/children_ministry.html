Children Ministry

Sunday School

Our Sunday School program teaches children the Word of God in a fun and engaging way. With Bible stories, songs, and crafts, we lay a strong spiritual foundation in the hearts of our little ones.

Bible Club

The Bible Club is an after-school spiritual program that encourages kids to explore the Bible more deeply. Through games, memory verses, and interactive lessons, children grow in their faith and love for God.

Goshen Pre-School and Day Care

Goshen Pre-School and Day Care is a Christ-centered learning environment where children receive quality education and care. We nurture their academic, spiritual, and social growth, preparing them for a bright future.
