Outreach Ministry

Missions

We are committed to fulfilling the Great Commission through local and international mission work. Our missions team travels to unreached and underserved areas to share the Gospel and demonstrate Christâ€™s love through acts of service.

Evangelism (Door-to-Door)

Our Door-to-Door Evangelism team boldly takes the message of salvation directly to homes in our neighborhoods. We share the Good News face-to-face, pray for households, and invite them to experience the love of Christ within our community.

Crusades

We organize powerful evangelistic crusades that gather people from all walks of life to hear the life-changing message of Jesus Christ. These open-air events are filled with praise, preaching, and miracles, drawing many to the Kingdom of God.

Discipleship Classes

Our Discipleship Classes are designed to nurture new believers and equip them for spiritual growth. We provide solid biblical foundations, spiritual mentorship, and guidance for those seeking to walk closely with Christ.

Jesus Filming (Cinema)

Through Jesus Film outreaches, we present the Gospel in a compelling and visual format using cinema. This method is especially effective in communities with limited access to traditional preaching, drawing many to Christ through storytelling.
